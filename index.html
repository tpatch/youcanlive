South Dakota. You Can Really Live Here.

Have you heard? Thousands of people submitted applications to be the first to colonize Mars. The air isn’t breathable. The surface is uninhabitable. And once they’re there…they can NEVER come back.

That got us wondering. Why die on Mars? When you can LIVE in South Dakota.

We've got plenty of job openings and all the food, water and oxygen you need! Keep reading and see why South Dakota is WAY better than Mars.

Find a fulfilling career and climb the ladder faster with more opportunities and fewer obstacles to overcome (we have the 4th best job market in the country). South Dakota is in the Midwest but our companies compete in the global marketplace, from making scoreboards and cheese to building robotic technology and conducting breakthrough medical and bioscience research.
